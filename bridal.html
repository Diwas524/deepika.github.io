About Us

हरेक बेहुलीको अनुहारमा एउटा अलग कहानी हुन्छ — हाम्रो कलाले त्यस कहानीलाई सुन्दर बनाउँछ।

After years of bridal work across Kathmandu, Lalitpur, and Pokhara, बेहुली Makeup has had the honour of being part of over 200 weddings. Each look is crafted individually — from deep traditional red sindoor glows to modern dewy finishes — always guided by the bride's personality, complexion, and the rituals ahead.

Our focus is on long-lasting, HD-ready makeup that photographs beautifully and stays flawless through every ritual — from the morning Mehendi to the evening reception. We use premium waterproof products tailored to Nepal's climate and skin tones.
